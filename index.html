All about Water

Water, a vital substance for all known forms of life, covers about 71% of Earth's surface and is predominantly found in oceans, lakes, rivers, and ice formations. It is unique due to its ability to exist in three states: solid (ice), liquid, and gas (vapor), playing a crucial role in the world's weather and climate systems. Additionally, water is essential for human consumption, agriculture, and industry, serving as a solvent in which many minerals, gases, and organic substances can be dissolved.
